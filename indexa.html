History of the Church

The original name of the church was called FOCCUS meaning Fellowship of the Celestial Chruch University Student. However, after the dissolution of the old FOCCUS (Fellowship of the Celestial Chruch University Student),Few Celastial Student formed the students'Fellowship, LAUTECH chapter in 1997 which later become a parish in 2008. The Church become a parish on August 16,th 2008 and the name of the Parish was called CELESTIAL CHURCH OF CHRIST STUDENT' PARISH, LADOKE AKINTOLA UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY, OGBOMOSO, OYO STATE. After then the Chruch has a Blessed name which is the motto of the church "THE PECULIAR PEOPLE" drawn from 1 peter 2:9.

NOTE:- The Church"s (CCCSPL) Anniversary is celebrated on the 16 th of August every year.

The aim and objectives of the parish

  1. To propagate the Gospel of Jesus Christ on the campus of LAUTECH and far beyond all over the world.
  2. To bring Chirstians/believers together under the umbrella of the Celestial Church for spiritual growth and salvation of souls.
  3. To cater for the physical, academic, moral and spiritual needs of members of the parish and ensuring their self/potential discovery.
  4. To fulfill the vision of the Celestial Church of Christ round OGBOMOSO township and beyond.
  5. To raise true disciples of the Lord Jesus Christ and ensure members maintain good communion-ship/Followship with the Lord Jesus Christ in Preparation for his (Christ) second coming.

The hierarchy (Organogram) of how the Chruch is Structure.

DIFFERENT UNIT

Departments present in the Church and what each units or departments demand from their members.

BLBLE UNIT

Equipped to give biblical guidance to and for all church members.In our Chruch it provides necessary encouragement to every member's of the Chruch.

PRAYER UNIT

Equip the church in the Place of prayer, by standing in the gap as either an intercessor, prayer warrior or to inculcate and imbible the prayerful christ like culture in the members of the parish.

EVANGELISM UNIT

Represent in recruiting and carrying out the mandate to the outside world beyond the church.

USHERING AND DECORATION UNIT

Responsible for coordination during service and beautifying the church with colourful design when needed.

CHORAL UNIT

Leads the church in worship, praise and other spiritual ministration and also represent the Church in any musical outings.

WELFARE UNIT

Consider the welfarism of the church member as priority for both academic and spiritual engagement.

TECHNICAL UNIT

Responsible for all mechanical and electrical materials/appliances and other related instruments regarding the church.

ACADEMIC UNIT

Equip the church members with solid academic understanding through tutorial,video tutorial ,books or any other academic aids.

LIBRARY UNIT

Feeds the whole church with academic, spiritual, moral and ethical books.

CATERING UNIT

Cater for Church members, through provision of internal resource material and training of individual in home-management.

PUBLICITY UNIT

Reponsible for information,media and public representation.

DRAMA UNIT

Engage drama as a tools to edify and minister in recruiting souls for the kingdom of God.

Criteria For joining or Functioning in any unit

  1. Every intending member must be a Christian or a Convert.
  2. He/She must be a student of LAUTECH
  3. Must have the zeal, passion and aim to serve God with his/her ability and be ready to abide by the rules of C.C.C and the Parish.
  4. He/She must have Passed through the Foundation school (and be issued his/her certificate) and other available biblical/spiritual trainings or classes organized by the Parish.
